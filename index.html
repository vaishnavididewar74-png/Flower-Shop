🌷 MEET THE FLORIST

Cultivating Beauty in Every Bloom

Welcome to LARANA FLORIST. Our passion for floral design goes far beyond just putting stems in a vase. We believe that every flower tells a story—of love, of comfort, of celebration, or of quiet remembrance. Every morning, we hand-select the freshest, most vibrant blossoms from local farms to ensure your arrangement arrives smelling incredible and lasting beautifully. From elegant, minimalist modern designs to lush, wild European-style bouquets, we craft each piece with artistry, intention, and love.
